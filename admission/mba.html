MBA (Business Analytics)

CUET-PG • 2 Years • Postgraduate Programme

Program Overview

Eligibility Criteria

  • Bachelor’s degree in any discipline (minimum 3 years)
  • General/OBC: 50% aggregate or equivalent CGPA
  • SC/ST/PwD: 45% aggregate
  • Admission strictly through CUET-PG examination

Program Details

  • Degree: Master of Business Administration (Business Analytics)
  • Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
  • Total Credits: ~100 credits
  • Industry-aligned analytics curriculum (AI/ML + Management)

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is CUET-PG mandatory?

Yes, admission is strictly through CUET-PG as per university guidelines.

2. Can non-engineering students apply?

Yes. Graduates from any discipline with the required percentage can apply.

3. Does the program include internships?

Yes, internships and industry projects are strongly encouraged in Semester 3 & 4.

4. What tools/software will students learn?

Python, R, SQL, Power BI/Tableau, Excel, ML libraries, and Big Data tools.

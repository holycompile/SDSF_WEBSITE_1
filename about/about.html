Mandatory Admission Prerequisites

Eligibility Criteria

Candidates must meet the following minimum academic requirements to be considered for admission through the CUET UG examination:

  • Required Stream: Higher Secondary (10+2) with Science stream and Mathematics as a subject from a recognized Board.
  • General/OBC: At least 50% marks in aggregate or an equivalent grade.
  • SC/ST/DA: 45% marks in aggregate or an equivalent grade for SC/ST and Differently Abled (DA) category candidates.
  • Entrance Test: Admission is strictly through the CUET UG examination.

Program Details

This program offers an integrated pathway to advanced technical roles, focusing on both software and analytical mastery.

  • Degree Awarded: Master of Technology (Dual Degree) in Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • Duration: 5 Years Integrated Program (2024-29 structure)
  • Note: Course contents are subject to modification from time to time in accordance with University Ordinance No. 14.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is this program equivalent to a standard B.Tech + M.Tech?

Yes, it integrates the undergraduate foundation and postgraduate specialization into a single, comprehensive 5-year dual degree program for accelerated qualification.

2. What is the required background in Mathematics?

Mathematics as a subject in the Higher Secondary (10+2) Science stream is a mandatory requirement for all applicants.

3. What is the final admission channel?

Admission is strictly processed based on the candidate's performance in the CUET UG entrance examination.
